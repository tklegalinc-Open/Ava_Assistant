## Deal Hunting Strategy

**Best nights for deals:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
**What to look for:**
- Prix fixe / table d'hôte menus
- Industry nights (Monday/Tuesday common)
- Happy hour extensions
- Seasonal promotions

**Sources to monitor:**
- Instagram accounts of target restaurants
- MTL Blog, Eater Montreal
- RestoMontreal deals section
- OpenTable/Resy specials
- La Presse food section
