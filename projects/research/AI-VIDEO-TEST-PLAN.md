# AI Video Ads Test Plan
*$200 budget, 2-week test*

---

## Objective

Test AI-generated video ads for TK Legal or Suite 77 to validate:
1. Quality is good enough for paid ads
2. Cost-per-video is sustainable
3. Performance vs. static image ads

---

## Recommended Tool Stack

### Option A: Kling AI (from the LinkedIn post)
- **What:** Text-to-video and image-to-video generation
- **Best for:** Realistic scenes, product shots, lifestyle content
- **Pricing:** Credit-based (need to sign up to confirm exact costs)
- **Sign up:** [klingai.com](https://klingai.com)

### Option B: Runway ML (established player)
- **What:** Gen-4.5 video generation, high quality
- **Pricing:** 
  - Standard: $12/month (625 credits = ~25 seconds of Gen-4.5)
  - Pro: $28/month (2250 credits = ~90 seconds)
  - Unlimited: $76/month (unlimited at relaxed rate)
- **Best for:** High-quality short clips, cinematic look
- **Sign up:** [runwayml.com](https://runwayml.com)

### Option C: Synthesia (talking head)
- **What:** AI avatar spokesperson videos
- **Pricing:** $18/month starter (10 min/month)
- **Best for:** Educational content, explainers (good for TK Legal tips)
- **Sign up:** [synthesia.io](https://synthesia.io)

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Cost per video | <$5 | Validates scalability |
| Video quality | "Would I post this?" | Subjective but real |
| CPM (cost per 1000 views) | <$15 | Industry benchmark |
| CTR (click-through rate) | >1% | Shows engagement |
| Video completion rate | >25% | Content resonates |
