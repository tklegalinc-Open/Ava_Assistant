## Tier 1: Must-Attend

### JPM Healthcare Conference
- **When:** January 13-16, 2026 (typically)
- **Where:** San Francisco, CA
- **Why attend:** Premier investor/BD event, deal-making central
- **Lead time:** Hotels book 6+ months out, meetings scheduled weeks ahead
- **Status:** ⚠️ Already passed if standard dates — did you attend?
- **Cost:** ~$5,000-8,000 (hotel + travel + meals)

### DCAT Week
- **When:** March 23-26, 2026 (typical late March)
- **Where:** New York, NY
- **Why attend:** CDMO/CMO focused, perfect for manufacturing positioning
- **Lead time:** Meeting requests 4-6 weeks ahead
- **Budget for:** ~$3,000-5,000
- **Prep needed:** Target company list, pitch deck updates

### BIO International Convention
- **When:** June 2-5, 2026 (estimated)
- **Where:** Varies (2025 was Boston, 2026 TBD)
- **Why attend:** Largest biotech partnering event, 15,000+ attendees
- **Decision:** Justify ROI — expensive, broad, can be unfocused
- **Budget for:** ~$8,000-12,000 if attending
- **Alternative:** Skip and focus on targeted outreach

### CPhI Europe (or CPhI North America)
- **When:** October 2026 (Europe typically Oct, NA varies)
- **Where:** Europe location TBD (Frankfurt, Barcelona common)
- **Why attend:** Manufacturing focus, European partnerships
- **Budget for:** ~$6,000-10,000 including transatlantic travel

## Pre-Conference Checklist

**6 weeks before:**
- [ ] Identify target companies/contacts
- [ ] Update pitch deck and materials
- [ ] Begin meeting request outreach
- [ ] Book travel and hotel

**2 weeks before:**
- [ ] Confirm all scheduled meetings
- [ ] Prepare company-specific talking points
- [ ] Print materials (business cards, leave-behinds)
- [ ] Review competitive landscape updates

**Post-conference (within 1 week):**
- [ ] Send follow-up emails (within 48 hours ideal)
- [ ] Log all contacts and conversations
- [ ] Identify hot leads for immediate follow-up
- [ ] Debrief notes — what worked, what didn't
