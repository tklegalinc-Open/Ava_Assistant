*Countering shiny object syndrome since 2026*

## The Problem

Tommy has 10+ ventures, limited time, and the brain of a strategist who sees opportunity everywhere. Every shiny object looks like it could be the big one.

**The cost:** Diluted focus, slow progress on things that matter, energy spent on things that don't.

---

## The Framework

### Before Starting Anything New, Ask:

**1. Does this serve a Tier 1 priority?**
- TK Legal (cash flow)
- Nualtis (current role, income)
- TKYM Excavation (2026 revenue goal)

If no â†’ it's probably a distraction.

**2. What's the realistic time investment?**
- Hours this week?
- Ongoing commitment?
- Is this a one-time task or a new open loop?

**3. What am I NOT doing if I do this?**
Every yes is a no to something else. Name it.

**4. Can this wait 90 days?**
If it's still exciting in 90 days, it's probably real. If not, you dodged a bullet.

**5. What's the minimum viable test?**
Can you validate with 4 hours instead of 40? Do that first.
