## Production Workflow

### Sunday Session Structure
1. **Hour 1:** Review week, capture raw ideas, outline long-form piece
2. **Hour 2:** Draft long-form content, create short-form derivatives
3. **Hour 3:** Schedule content, prep visuals, batch social posts

### AI-Assisted Tasks
- Outline generation from voice notes
- First draft expansion
- Repurposing long-form → short-form
- Headline/hook variations
- Image prompt generation
